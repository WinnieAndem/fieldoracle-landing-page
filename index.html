WHAT IS FIELDORACLE?

Fieldoracle is a global, freelance marketplace application connecting researchers with freelance data collectors on demand to assist with data collection for any research and can also serve as research assistants in the research location.

As a freelancer on Fieldoracle, you can create an expert profile that is searchable by researchers looking for data collectors with specific research expertise to collect data in their community. When invited, you may chat with project owners to clarify project details, submit proposals for projects, communicate and share files with project owners, and submit invoices to get paid.
